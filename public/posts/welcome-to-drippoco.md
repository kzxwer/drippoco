# File-Based Publishing

This blog uses markdown files in `public/posts/**`.

## How to publish

1. Go to `/create-article`
2. Write your article with markdown preview
3. Click **Download article file**
4. Move the downloaded `.md` into `public/posts/` (or any subfolder)
5. Deploy again

Your article will appear on the homepage automatically.
